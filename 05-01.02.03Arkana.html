ARKANA 2026

On May 1, 2, and 3, we look forward to seeing you in the Sacred Valley of the Incas, Peru, for a new edition of ARKANA.

This is not just a festival;
it is a sacred ritual and an offering to the land that still bears the traces of the Inca.

In the heart of the Andes,
under the eternal gaze of the Apus,
we will gather to remember, celebrate, and transform.

---

🌎 This year, we renew our commitment to the planet and the community:

✨ ARKANA will be 100% single-use plastic free.
✨ We will work exclusively with local producers, creating a direct and positive impact on the region.
✨ All attendees, including artists and guests, must accompany their tickets with a bag of non-perishable food, as part of our donation to the community—
strengthening our social commitment and raising awareness about inequalities.

---

🌿 ARKANA is a sanctuary of peace and harmony.

Here, hate speech and mistreatment based on sex, race, religion, or any other difference will not be tolerated.

The flags of peace and Tawantinsuyo will rise in solidarity with the Palestinian people.

Everyone is welcome— but we want to make our position clear and respected!

---

🌌 Get ready for three days of:

🔮 Profound psychedelic music
🔥 Transformative ceremonies
🎨 Visionary art
🌿 Ancestral wisdom

All within a temple of sound, among mountains and stars. ✨

---

ARKANA – not only a celebration… but a ritual for the soul.
